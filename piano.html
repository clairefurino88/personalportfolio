My Piano Career

Musical Perception

I started piano I was 5. It has since gone from a tedious chore to an immersive escape that I would not trade for anything else in the world. Throughout my piano career, music (initially about virtuosity) has gone from simply creating beautiful music to pouring myself into a language, with which I can connect to my audience.

Throughout my music education, I have been blessed with top-notch teachers. I am studying under Professor Thomas Schultz, and currently a member of the Young Artist Guild. And of course if it were not for my previous mentors - Professor Hans Boepple and Dr. Jed Galant - I would not be a fraction of the musician I am today.
