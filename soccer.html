My Soccer Career

Freestyle & Free Kicks

I started playing soccer when I was 5, but early on it was mostly my dad dragging me to practice. It was not until 8th grade when I fell in love with it. One of my biggest inspirations to this day is Joltter, a Finnish college dropout who spent his days making and editing free kick/freestyle videos.

So, like him, I began to make free kick videos (cut my own hair). It was around then that I became fascinated with soccer because of its technical beauty. There is something to be said about "fluency," a harmony between the ball, body, and mind that allows one to create a unique style.
